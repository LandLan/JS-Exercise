direction 定义文字排列方式(全兼容)
rtl     从右向左排列
ltr     从左向右排列
注意要配合unicode-bidi 一块使用

p:nth-of-type(1){direction:ltr;}

Life is too short to spend time regretting. If it's not the end, smile and keep on going. 生命太短，没时间留给遗憾。若不是终点，请微笑一直向前。

p:nth-of-type(2){direction:rtl;}

Life is too short to spend time regretting. If it's not the end, smile and keep on going. 生命太短，没时间留给遗憾。若不是终点，请微笑一直向前。

p:nth-of-type(3){direction:rtl; unicode-bidi:bidi-override;}

Life is too short to spend time regretting. If it's not the end, smile and keep on going. 生命太短，没时间留给遗憾。若不是终点，请微笑一直向前。


text-overflow     定义省略文本的处理方式
clip     无省略号
ellipsis     省略号 (注意配合overflow:hidden和white-space:nowrap一块使用)

p:nth-of-type(4){text-overflow:clip; overflow:hidden; white-space:nowrap;}

Life is too short to spend time regretting. If it's not the end, smile and keep on going. 生命太短，没时间留给遗憾。若不是终点，请微笑一直向前。

p:nth-of-type(5){text-overflow:ellipsis; overflow:hidden; white-space:nowrap;}

Life is too short to spend time regretting. If it's not the end, smile and keep on going. 生命太短，没时间留给遗憾。若不是终点，请微笑一直向前。
